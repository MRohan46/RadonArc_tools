About RadonArc

RadonArc is a collection of free online tools designed to make everyday tasks easier. Whether you need to calculate your age, convert units, generate a QR code, or transform file formats, we've built simple utilities that just work.

No bloated interfaces. No intrusive ads. No data collection. Just clean, fast tools that respect your time and privacy.

Every tool runs entirely in your browser, meaning your data never touches our servers. We don't track what you do, we don't sell your information, and we don't show you ads based on your activity.

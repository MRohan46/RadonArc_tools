Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this age calculator?

This calculator is 100% accurate. It accounts for leap years and calculates your exact age down to the minute.

Is my birth date stored or tracked?

No. All calculations happen in your browser. Your birth date is never sent to our servers or stored anywhere.

What is the "Calculate Age As Of" field for?

This optional field lets you calculate what your age was or will be on a specific date. Leave it empty to calculate your current age.

Can I calculate someone else's age?

Yes! Just enter their birth date. This tool can calculate anyone's age.

Common Questions

What's the difference between DEG and RAD?

DEG (degrees) and RAD (radians) are two units for measuring angles. Use DEG for most calculations (360° in a circle). RAD is used in advanced mathematics (2π radians in a circle).

How do I calculate sin(30)?

Make sure you're in DEG mode, type 30, then press the sin button. The result will be 0.5.

What does ln mean?

ln is the natural logarithm, which is log base e (where e ≈ 2.71828). It's the inverse of eˣ.

Can I use parentheses for complex calculations?

Yes! Use the ( and ) buttons to group operations. For example: (5+3)×2 = 16
